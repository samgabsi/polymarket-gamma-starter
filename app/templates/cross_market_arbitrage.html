Cross-Market Arbitrage

Polymarket, Kalshi, and future venue snapshots are normalized, matched deterministically, scored for equivalence, and reviewed for net margin after fees, slippage, liquidity, and resolution risk.

Detection/review only. Candidates are not guaranteed profits and do not place orders, cancel orders, approve trades, or arm live trading.

Review actions persist local audit rows only. Use them to mark candidates for human follow-up, not to execute across venues.
