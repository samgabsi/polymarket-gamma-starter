What “adjust odds” means here

The app adjusts its internal draft model fair probability and fair odds for research/review. It does not alter external market odds or submit any order.

  • Source weighting does not prove truth.
  • Corroboration does not prove certainty.
  • AI summaries can be wrong and must be reviewed.
  • Accepted adjustments update review context only, not live trading state.
  • Raw AI movement, evidence-weighted movement, and final risk-controlled movement are tracked separately.
