# Live Risk Controls

Version: 1.0.0-real

Manual live submit/cancel remain protected by default-off configuration, kill switch, authorization, final confirmation, market allowlists, max order notional, stale-source checks, and audit logging. Autonomous live trading remains blocked.

Risk checks are local controls; they do not guarantee fills, liquidity, price quality, or profit. This software is not financial advice.


## v1.1.0 checklist integration

The live readiness checklist surfaces risk blockers such as missing allowlists, missing max notional, kill switch state, missing final confirmation, and missing credentials before any live operation.
