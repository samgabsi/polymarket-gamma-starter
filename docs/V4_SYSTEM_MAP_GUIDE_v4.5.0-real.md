# System Map Guide - v4.5.0-real

The system map includes AI Edge, market recommendation surfaces, market-family ranking, aliases, route safety metadata, and no-live-mutation posture.

## v4.5 Additions

- AI Edge market-row routes.
- Market edge legend and per-market recommendation APIs.
- Market family ranking summary APIs.
- Favorite ranking does not imply edge metadata.
- Edge recommendations are review-only metadata.

Route aliases remain convenience links. They do not place orders, cancel orders, approve trades, or bypass backend gates.


## Safety and Scope

All edge, AI Edge, evidence, calibration, family-ranking, and recommendation output is draft research only. It is not financial advice, not a trade approval, and not an executable order. The release does not place orders, cancel orders, arm live trading, disable read-only mode, disable the kill switch, or let AI bypass backend gates. Live controls, paper controls, approval checkboxes, typed confirmation phrases, warning acknowledgements, audit logging, emergency controls, and kill-switch controls remain authoritative.
