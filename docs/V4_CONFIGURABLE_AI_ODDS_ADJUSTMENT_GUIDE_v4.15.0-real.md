# Configurable AI Odds Adjustment Guide - v4.15.0-real

The AI odds adjustment workflow remains the v4.12 review-only implementation in this release.

## Current Behavior

- Browser actions redirect with operator feedback.
- Saved draft adjustments open a detail page.
- Accept to review context, reject, and archive actions persist local review records.
- Raw AI adjustment, evidence-weighted adjustment, final risk-controlled adjustment, cap decision, confidence, and recommended YES/NO side remain visible.
- Web search remains disabled/config-gated by default.

## Safety

AI odds adjustments are draft research context only. They do not change Polymarket prices, approve trades, place orders, cancel orders, arm live trading, or provide financial advice.
